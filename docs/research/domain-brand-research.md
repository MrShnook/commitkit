# CommitKit — Domain & Brand Research

*Completed: 2026-03-01*

## Brand Name Analysis

### Why "CommitKit" Is the Right Name

**"Commit" is THE word in college recruitment.** When an athlete chooses their school, they "commit" — it's a verbal commit, a signed commit, a de-commit. This word is used daily by every family, coach, and recruiting service in the space. It's the single most emotionally charged word in the process.

**"Kit" signals utility.** It's a toolkit, a starter kit, everything-you-need-in-one-place. It positions the product as practical and approachable — not a bloated enterprise platform.

**Brand strengths:**
- ✅ Two syllables, easy to spell and say
- ✅ Domain-relevant — instantly communicates "recruiting commitment tools"
- ✅ Family-friendly tone (vs. corporate-sounding alternatives)
- ✅ Naturally extensible: "Your CommitKit" / "Build your kit" / "Kit up for recruiting season"
- ✅ Unique — no direct competitor uses this name
- ✅ Works across sports (not soccer-specific, enabling future expansion)

## Brand Identity Direction

### Positioning
- **Not** a recruiting marketplace (that's NCSA, FieldLevel)
- **Not** a coach's tool (that's Hudl, SportsRecruits)
- **IS** the family's command center for the recruiting process

### Tone & Voice
- Confident but approachable (parent to parent, not salesperson)
- Empowering ("You've got this. We'll organize the chaos.")
- Not corporate ("We don't do jargon. Neither do you.")
- Slightly urgent — recruiting has deadlines, families feel that pressure

### Visual Direction (for design exploration)
- **Colors:** Deep navy/dark blue (trust, sports) + bright accent (energy, action — amber? green?)
- **Not:** Neon/gamer vibes, enterprise gray, kiddie cartoon
- **Feel:** Modern sports dashboard meets family-friendly app
- **Inspiration:** Linear (clean, focused), Notion (organized), Strava (sports/achievement)
- **Logo concept:** Stylized "CK" monogram or shield/crest motif

### Tagline Candidates
1. "Every step to your commit." — Journey + power word
2. "Organize the road to college sports." — Descriptive, SEO-friendly
3. "Your recruiting command center." — Positions as the HUB
4. "From first email to signed NLI." — Concrete process
5. "The toolkit for recruiting families." — Direct, audience-clear

**Recommended:** "Every step to your commit."
