# CommitKit — Market Sizing Estimate

*Research completed: 2026-03-01*

## Bottom-Up Market Sizing

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)

**US High School Soccer Players (2023-24):** ~850,000
- Boys: ~467,500 (55%)
- Girls: ~382,500 (45%)
- Growth: ~3% YoY from 2022

**Actively Pursuing College Soccer:**
- Boys: 9.74% transition rate → ~45,500 per year
- Girls: 11.29% transition rate → ~43,200 per year
- **Total pursuing:** ~88,700 players/year

**But the recruitment PROCESS involves 2-3 years per player:**
- Families start sophomore year (age 15-16)
- Peak activity: junior + senior year
- **Active recruitment pipeline at any time:** ~88,700 × 2.5 years = **~220,000 families**

### D1 Odds (the fear that drives spending)
- Boys D1: 0.9% (108:1 odds)
- Girls D1: 2.4% (41:1 odds)
- D2, D3, NAIA, JUCO have better odds but less visibility

## Top-Down Cross-Checks

### Youth Sports Industry
- US youth sports: **$28B+ industry** (2024)
- College recruitment services estimated at $500M-1B segment
- NCSA alone: estimated $150-200M+ revenue (3M+ users, premium packages)

### Family Spending on Recruitment
- Families spend **$5K-20K+** on the recruitment process annually
  - Travel teams/clubs: $3K-8K/year
  - Camps/showcases: $1K-5K/year
  - NCSA/recruiting services: $1K-4K/year
  - Video production: $500-2K
  - Travel to campus visits: $2K-5K
- **A $15-25/mo tool is a rounding error** in the overall recruitment budget

### Comparable SaaS Benchmarks
- **HubSpot CRM** (professional contacts): Free → $50/mo → $800/mo
- **Monday.com** (project management): $8-16/user/mo
- **Trello** (Kanban boards): Free → $10/mo
- CommitKit at $15-25/mo is **well within willingness-to-pay** for families spending $10K+/year on recruitment

## Multi-Sport Expansion (Future)

Soccer is the beachhead. The recruitment process is nearly identical across:
- ⚽ Soccer (~850K HS players)
- 🏀 Basketball (~540K)
- 🏈 Football (~1M)
- ⚾ Baseball (~480K)
- 🏐 Volleyball (~450K)
- 🏃 Track & Field (~1M)
- 🎾 Tennis (~200K)

**Multi-sport TAM:** 4.5M+ HS athletes × 10% pursuing college = **450K families**
At 5% capture × $19/mo × 12 = **$51M+/year**

## Key Takeaway

The market is real, the pain is acute, and families are already spending $10K+/year on the process. A $19/mo CRM that reduces chaos and stress is a no-brainer purchase for this audience. NCSA's bad reputation creates a massive opening for a trustworthy, transparent, affordable alternative.

**Soccer-first, expand to all sports once the product is proven.**
